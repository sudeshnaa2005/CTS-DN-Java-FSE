🌟 Community Event Portal Help Center

Everything you need to know about using the portal.

🏠 Home Section

The Home page displays community announcements, upcoming events, and important updates from the city council.

🎉 Events Section

Browse community activities such as marathons, music festivals, food fairs, sports events, and environmental drives.

📝 Registration

Fill out the registration form with your details. After submission, a confirmation message will be displayed.

📍 Nearby Events

Click "Find Nearby Events" to allow location access. The portal will display your coordinates and help identify events close to your area.

💡 Helpful Tips

• Double-click event images to enlarge them.
• Your preferred event is saved automatically.
• Use the Clear Preferences button to reset saved data.
• Keep your browser updated for the best experience.
